FAQ

What is MegaGamee?

MegaGamee is a free, play-for-fun mini-game arcade. Pick a game, beat your high score, share it on Telegram. Four games to start: Dino Runner, Flappy, Snake, and Stack Tower.

Is this real-money gambling?

No. There’s nothing to buy, no deposits, no winnings. Every game is purely for fun — just you vs. your own high score.

How do I play?

Tap any game card above to launch it. Each game has its own quick controls shown on the start screen — tap or swipe on mobile, arrow keys or space on desktop.

Where are my high scores saved?

Locally in your browser. They persist between visits on the same device, but clearing your browser data will reset them. There’s no account to sign up for.

Does it work on phone?

Yes — mobile-first. Every game is designed for portrait phone screens, with tap and swipe controls. Works in the Telegram in-app browser too.

How do I share my score?

For now: screenshot the game-over screen and post it in the Telegram channel (tap “Join Telegram” at the bottom). A built-in share button is on the roadmap.
